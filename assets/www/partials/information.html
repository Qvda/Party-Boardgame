How to win

You must gain a certain amount of points to win the game. There are a couple of ways to gain points:

  • Move a square: +1
  • Throw a one: +1
  • Win a minigame: +5
  • Stop on a yellow 'Points' square: +5
  • Stop on a red 'Points' square: -3
  • Stop on the green 'Start' square: +10
  • Stop on the blue 'Jackpot' square: the jackpot

There are always at least 5 points in the jackpot. Every time a player stops on a red square the 3 points lost will be added to the jackpot.
